Explain the process of photosynthesis.

AI Tutor

Of course! Photosynthesis is the process used by plants, algae, and certain bacteria to harness energy from sunlight and turn it into chemical energy. Here's a simple breakdown:

Key Ingredients:

  • Sunlight
  • Water (H₂O)
  • Carbon Dioxide (CO₂)

The Process:

  1. Light-Dependent Reactions: The plant's chlorophyll absorbs sunlight.
  2. Light-Independent Reactions (Calvin Cycle): This energy is used to convert CO₂ and water into glucose (sugar/food).

Oxygen is released as a byproduct. It's essential for life on Earth!
